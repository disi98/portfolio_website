Why Blue Marine

Destination Experts

With our deep knowledge of the region, we have unrivaled expertise in East Africa's breathtaking destinations. Whether you dream of witnessing the Great Migration in the Maasai Mara, exploring the mystical landscapes of Amboseli National Park, or unwinding on the stunning beaches of the Kenyan coast, we have the insights and experience to make it happen.

Personalized Itineraries

Kenya is renowned for its abundant wildlife. Join us on thrilling game drives and safaris led by expert guides, where you can witness the iconic Big Five, marvel at the vibrant birdlife, and immerse yourself in the wonders of nature

Cultural Immersion

Immerse yourself in the vibrant cultures of East Africa. We offer opportunities to engage with local communities, learn about traditional customs and rituals, and gain a deeper understanding of the rich heritage that makes this region so special

Professional Guides

Our knowledgeable and passionate guides are the backbone of your journey. They possess a deep love for their country and will share captivating stories, provide expert insights, and ensure your safety and comfort throughout your travels

Sustainable Tourism

Bluemarine Tours and Travels is committed to sustainable and responsible tourism practices. We strive to minimize our environmental impact, support local communities, and promote conservation efforts to preserve the natural and cultural treasures of East Africa for future generations.

Exceptional Customer Service

Your satisfaction is our top priority. From the moment you contact us to the conclusion of your journey, our friendly and attentive team will be at your service, ensuring that every aspect of your trip is meticulously planned and executed to perfection

Join us at Bluemarine Tours and Travels and embark on an extraordinary adventure that will create memories to last a lifetime. Let us be your trusted partner in discovering the magic of East Africa and beyond.
